Getting started with Odoo

So, you are looking to implement an ERP in your company and you find Odoo apealling. Opensource software is undoubtly the way to go and Odoo is the best option available.

Now, how should you get started with Odoo? I get lot’s of people that wishes to implement Odoo in their business but they don’t know where to start. At this point, if you feel you cannot move further without help, then you should get professional training or consulting. Best advice i can give you is to get a good Odoo demo and training sessions to understand how the base product can adapt to your business before you start thinking about customization.
